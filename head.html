Start - Mimic Queen

  • Visit the Giza shop: Dark, Onion Arrows x2
  • Kill Dustia x3 before you go to Dalan's
    • Vaan/Penelo Unarmed Dagger
    Armor 20/13 42/38
    No Armor 28/20 56/51
  • Save at the start of Garamsythe, and reset until you see Razorfin
  • Kill Garchimacera and Ghost in Garamsythe
  • Kill Battery Mimic, Flan and Mimic in Barheim
  • Save before Mimic Queen for Seitengrat manipulation
  • After Mimic Queen, run to South Bank Village and TP to Rabanastre

    • Bhujerba - Leviathan

  • Get Basch, start Thextera and get Balthier and Fran
  • Go to Westersand, kill Thextera (Fire)
  • Get the Diamond Armlet (Chance: 25, RNG Position: 8)
  • Back to Rabanastre, fly to Bhujerba, and head to Lhusu
  • Pick up Silence and Numerology, and kill Aerieel if it shows up
  • Get back to Aerodrome and save, then manipulate two Seitengrat (Position 544)
  • After 2nd Seitengrat, head out to East Gate and run/chocobo to the camp to get start Flowering Cactoid
  • Kill Flowering Cactoid, try to spawn Ripe Rampager, then back to Dantro and talk to him twice
  • Run to Nalbina, TP to Bhujerba, finish Leviathan

    • Post-Leviathan - Vossler

  • Westersand Shop: Cura

    • Rabanastre

  • TP to Rabanastre, start and kill Wraith (and turn it in)
  • Go to the Sandsea, turn in Thextera and pick up 2 hunts
  • Start Wyvern Lord in weapon shop, then TP back to Westersand

    • Sandsea Trip

  • Pick up Sleep, Infuse and Reflect in Ogri-Yensa
  • Chain 100 Urutan-Yensa and kill Urutan Exile
  • Pick up Balance and Embroidered Tippet in Nam-Yensa
  • Kill Wyvern Lord and pick up 2000 gil chest
  • After the first waystone in Raithwall Tomb, use the center waystone to unlock center waystone
  • Pick up Dispel, Vanish and Shades of Black
  • Hit the TC on the way out (or in)

    • Post-Vossler - Stilshrine

  • After Vossler fight, go to the Clan Hall to pick up Cluckatrice and Rocktoise
  • Get hunts in Sandsea (, and turn in Wyvern Lord), then Southgate to continue
  • Pick up Haste in Eruyt and Bonecrusher in Henne, then kill Tiamat
  • Do a detour in Feywood to pick up Embroidered Tippet, then go to MBO

    • Stilshrine

  • Take off Chocobo after the crystal and kill Twintania and pick up Revive
  • In the next area, kill Emperor Aevis and Yeti
  • Put Dawn Shard on Vaan and hit the pedestal
  • Check the time you hit the first pedestal and add 30 minutes for Negalmuur spawn
  • Pick up Countdown, then kill Vinuskar and Mateus
  • Go to the secret area, zone in and out for Matriarch Bomb
  • Burn the rest of the 30 minutes farming gil chest as much as possible
  • Kill Negalmuur after it summons Ghast
  • Kill Bergan and talk to the guy for Stilshrine stone, then TP to Bhujerba

    • Bhujerba - Giza Wet

  • Go to Khus Skygrounds, start Rocktoise and Nidhogg
  • Go to Lhusu and kill Rocktoise and Nidhogg
  • Kill Aerieel if it spawns, and make sure you've picked up Silence and Numerology
  • Turn in Nidhogg and Rocktoise
  • Save at Bhujerba Aerodrome and manipulate Seitengrat, and buy more than 30 Teleport Stones

    • Giza Wet

  • Grab Chocobo at Southgate, note the time you entered Giza Wet (Giza Cycle: 1 hour wet and 2 hours dry, swapping at the top of the hour)
  • Talk to Sadeen for Croakadile, then run to the SW zone for Croakadile
  • On the way back, hit 4 Withered Trees (SE of zone line, NE in next, SE in Village, NW of Village)
  • Kill Rain Dancer if it spawns on the way
  • Back to Rabanstre and TP to Estersand

    • Estersand Side Quests

  • Turn in Cactus Flower, zone out and in then take the boat
  • Cross to North Bank Village and proceed the event, then talk to Dantro's wife again
  • Find flower behind the house and go back to NBV to finish the sidequest

    • Estersand Rares

  • Back in SBV, talk to Dantro's wife then get Semclam Shell by the coast, and turn it in
  • Go outside and kill stuff (or do licences) for 3 minutes, then kill Greeden and Ichthon
  • Kill the groups of Cockatrices and pick up Poison in the next zone until Nekhbet spawns
  • In the next zone, try to spawn and kill Ripe Rampager
  • Go to the camp and get Nebralim, and doublezone to spawn Ripe Rampager if you haven't killed
  • Go towards Rabanatre, killing Wild Saurian then TP to Estersand
  • Turn in Nebralim and TP to Nalbina

    • Mosphoran - Ahriman

  • Enter Nalbina, and hit BB to get all the hunts, then start Atomos (Burrogh)
  • Take Chocobo, go out of way to Estersand to pick up Valeblossom Dew
  • Kill Wary Wolf if he spawns
  • In Mospho Town, TP to Estersand and turn in VBD (talk to wife twice), then continue towards Salikawoods
  • Next zone, kill Python, and hit the rocks on the right at the end
  • Kill Atomos in the next zone (probably outrange him for safety)

    • Salikawood - Sochen Cave Palace

  • Detour to TC in Salikawood before getting last 2 moogle huts and pick up Charm
  • In Phon Coast, hit the TC then do shops: Steel Mask etc.
  • Chocobo to Tchita TC, get Mandies hunt and hit TC
  • Pick up Blindga in Tchita then kill Mandies and Ahriman
  • Hit TC before Old Archades
